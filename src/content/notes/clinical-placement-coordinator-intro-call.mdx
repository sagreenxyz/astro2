## Introductory Call with Holly Underhill, Placement Coordinator

**Date/Time:** 1100 CDT  
**Contact:** Holly Underhill, Placement Coordinator — clpsprelicensure@wgu.edu

This note summarizes the scheduled introductory phone call with the WGU Clinical Learning and Placement Services (CLPS) team regarding lab and clinical placement for the BSN program.

---

### Clinical Structure Overview

Clinicals are supervised by **two roles**: a preceptor who is an employee of the clinical facility, and a WGU-affiliated clinical instructor. All clinical sites are located **within a 60-mile radius of the center of Kansas City**.

---

### Labs

There are **3 lab experiences** in the program, one of which is conducted virtually.

---

### Clinical Sessions

There are **7 clinical sessions** corresponding to the following courses:

1. Adult Health I
2. Adult Health II
3. Women's and Children's Health
4. Psychiatric and Mental Health
5. Community Health
6. Adult Health III
7. Transition to Practice
